--- SELECTOR & TARGETING CHEAT SHEET ---

1. @p - The nearest player (or yourself in Singleplayer)
2. @a - Every player currently in the world (Global)
3. @r - A random player from everyone online
4. <Username> - A specific player by their name

EXAMPLES:

/tp @a 0 64 0
(Teleports everyone to spawn)

/gamemode creative @r
(Pick a lucky winner for creative mode)

/setAttribute @a speed 1.5
(Increase walk speed for the whole server)

/clear @a apple
(Confiscate all apples from everyone)

/item property selected weight +1
/item modify selected {"onUse":"execute if data storage item.weight 10 run title @a title §6CRITICAL MASS REACHED"}
